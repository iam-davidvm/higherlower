Higher ⬆ Lower ⬇

What?

Higher Lower is a card game where you have to guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the current card.

You can guess by pressing the higher/lower buttons or by using your keyboard: arrow up or 'h' is higher, arrow down or 'l' is lower.

Goal

If you manage to guess everything correctly, you win. Else you lose.

Other rules

  • The ace card has a value of 1
  • If the next card has the same value, the game goes on
